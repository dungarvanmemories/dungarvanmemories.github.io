Dungarvan Links

Dungarvan Museum Society have an excellent collection of pictures and articles about Dungarvan. This is the main site for Dungarvan History. It contains more than 400 pages and is updated quite frequently. This site also contains useful tourist information. The Dungarvan Museum Society site was created by Deise Design

Desies is a journal concerned with the history and archeology of Co Waterford. This journal is still print based but the site gives a contents page for back copies of the journal.

Greg Fewer seems to be at the forefront of the move to put Waterford history on the internet.

The Rathgormack Home Page is a very informative site. Some of the picture on this site are a bit slow to load but they are worth the wait.

Donal Power has some very nice images of Dungarvan on his site.
